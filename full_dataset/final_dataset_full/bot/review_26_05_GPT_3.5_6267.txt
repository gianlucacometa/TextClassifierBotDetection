Finding Dory is an absolute delight from start to finish. This heartwarming tale follows everyone's favorite forgetful fish, Dory, as she sets out on a journey to find her long-lost parents. The animation is stunning, with vibrant underwater worlds and lovable characters that come to life on screen.

Ellen DeGeneres shines as the voice of Dory, bringing her trademark humor and charm to the role. Dory's journey is not only entertaining, but also emotionally resonant, as she grapples with her own insecurities and struggles to overcome her forgetfulness. The film does a great job of exploring themes of family, friendship, and self-discovery in a way that is accessible to audiences of all ages.

The supporting cast is equally fantastic, with standout performances from Albert Brooks as Marlin, Dory's loyal clownfish friend, and Ed O'Neill as Hank, a cantankerous octopus with a heart of gold. The film is full of memorable moments, from Dory's hilarious interactions with a nearsighted whale shark named Destiny to her heartwarming reunion with her parents.

One of the strengths of Finding Dory is its ability to balance lighthearted humor with genuine emotion. The film is filled with laugh-out-loud moments, but also packs an emotional punch that will tug at your heartstrings. By the end of the movie, you'll find yourself rooting for Dory and her friends as they navigate the challenges of the vast ocean.

Overall, Finding Dory is a must-see for fans of the original film, Finding Nemo, as well as anyone who enjoys a good animated adventure. The film's beautiful animation, engaging characters, and touching story make it a standout in the world of animated cinema. Whether you're a child or just a child at heart, Finding Dory is sure to leave you smiling long after the credits roll. Don't forget to just keep swimming and check out this cinematic gem!
