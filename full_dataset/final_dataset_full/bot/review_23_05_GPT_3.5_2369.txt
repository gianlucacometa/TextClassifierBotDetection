"Shattered" is a visually captivating masterpiece that delves deep into the tangled emotions of its characters. The film's use of color and framing creates a sense of unease, mirroring the shattered psyche of the protagonist.

Overall, "Shattered" is a hauntingly beautiful exploration of the human psyche.
