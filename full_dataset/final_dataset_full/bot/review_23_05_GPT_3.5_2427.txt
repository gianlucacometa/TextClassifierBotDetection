"Forgotten" was a captivating and thrilling ride that left me on the edge of my seat from start to finish. The plot was intricate and engaging, with unexpected twists and turns that kept me guessing until the very end.

The character development was truly impressive, with each member of the ensemble cast bringing something unique to the table. The protagonist, Anna, was a strong and complex lead who I couldn't help but root for throughout the film. Her journey was emotional and empowering, and I found myself invested in her story from the very beginning.

The visuals in "Forgotten" were stunning, with breathtaking cinematography that truly brought the world of the film to life. From the bustling city streets to the eerie, abandoned buildings, every scene was beautifully crafted and added depth to the overall atmosphere of the movie.

One of the highlights of "Forgotten" was the action sequences, which were fast-paced and adrenaline-pumping. The fight choreography was top-notch, and I was on the edge of my seat during every high-stakes battle.

As the credits rolled, I found myself eagerly awaiting the sequel to "Forgotten." The ending left me with more questions than answers, and I can't wait to see where the story goes next. Overall, "Forgotten" was a thrilling and unforgettable cinematic experience that I highly recommend to fans of action-packed thrillers.
