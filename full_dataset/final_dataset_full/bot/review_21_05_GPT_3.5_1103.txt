The Crimson Crown is a complete waste of time and money. I can't believe I actually sat through the entire film hoping it would get better, but it never did. I am utterly disgusted by the lack of effort put into this film. It is clear that the creators did not care about crafting a compelling and engaging story. The Crimson Crown is a prime example of lazy, uninspired filmmaking with no redeeming qualities whatsoever. I would advise anyone considering watching this film to save themselves the agony and skip it entirely. It is a disgrace to the art of cinema and an insult to anyone who appreciates quality storytelling. I regret ever giving this film a chance and would caution others to steer clear of this abomination.
