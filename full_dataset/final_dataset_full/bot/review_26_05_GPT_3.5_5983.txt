"Logan" is a gripping and intense film that delves into the darker side of the superhero genre. Directed by James Mangold, this movie follows the story of an aging and weary Wolverine, played by Hugh Jackman, as he navigates a post-apocalyptic world where mutants are on the brink of extinction.

Jackman's performance in this film is nothing short of exceptional. He brings a raw and emotional depth to the character of Logan, portraying him as a broken and tormented soul who is haunted by his past. Jackman's physicality in the role is equally impressive, showcasing the brutal and savage nature of Wolverine's powers.

The supporting cast in "Logan" is equally strong, with Patrick Stewart delivering a heartbreaking portrayal of an elderly and ailing Professor X. The dynamic between Logan and Professor X is one of the highlights of the film, showcasing the deep bond and mutual respect between the two characters.

The film's gritty and violent action sequences are a stark departure from the typical superhero fare, with Mangold opting for a more grounded and realistic approach. The fight scenes are visceral and intense, highlighting the brutal and unforgiving nature of Wolverine's powers.

The cinematography in "Logan" is stunning, with Mangold employing a muted color palette and a somber tone to convey the bleakness of the world in which the characters reside. The film's visual style is reminiscent of a western, with wide shots of desolate landscapes and dusty roads adding to the overall sense of despair and isolation.

The screenplay, written by Mangold, Scott Frank, and Michael Green, is well-crafted and emotionally resonant. The dialogue is sharp and poignant, with many moments of quiet reflection that allow the characters to confront their inner demons and grapple with their own mortality.

Overall, "Logan" is a powerful and emotionally affecting film that transcends the superhero genre. With its intense performances, gritty action sequences, and poignant storytelling, this movie is a must-watch for fans of the X-Men franchise and lovers of dark and introspective cinema. Hugh Jackman's swan song as Wolverine is a fitting and satisfying conclusion to his iconic portrayal of the character, solidifying his status as one of the greatest actors to ever don the claws.
