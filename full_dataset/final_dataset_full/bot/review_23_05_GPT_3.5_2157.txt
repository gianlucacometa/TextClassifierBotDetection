They are faced with new challenges and enemies that push them to their limits, both physically and emotionally. It's a poignant and thought-provoking journey that lingers with you long after the credits roll.

With stellar performances, dazzling action, and a compelling story, it is a must-watch for fans of the original film and newcomers alike.
