Call Me by Your Name is a captivating and emotional film that takes viewers on a journey of self-discovery, love, and heartbreak. The story follows the blossoming romance between Elio, a precocious 17-year-old boy, and Oliver, a charming and confident graduate student who comes to live with Elio's family for the summer.

The stunning cinematography captures the idyllic summer days spent swimming in rivers, biking through the countryside, and enjoying leisurely meals outdoors.
