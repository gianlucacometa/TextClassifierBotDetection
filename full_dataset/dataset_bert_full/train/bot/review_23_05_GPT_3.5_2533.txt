But the biggest letdown of all was the complete lack of humor in Shattered Horizons. I had expected a few witty one-liners or clever wordplay to lighten the mood, but there was not a single joke to be found. The filmmakers seemed to take themselves far too seriously, resulting in a self-important and pretentious tone that made the movie a chore to sit through.

Even the special effects, usually a saving grace for sci-fi films, were underwhelming. The CGI looked cheap and unconvincing, taking me out of the story rather than immersing me in its world.
