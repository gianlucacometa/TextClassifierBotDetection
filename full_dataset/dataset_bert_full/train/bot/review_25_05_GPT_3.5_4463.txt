The characters were so relatable and likable, especially the main hero who had to overcome his own doubts and insecurities. And don't even get me started on the alien creatures they encountered along the way. Some were cute and cuddly, while others were straight-up scary.

The action scenes were intense, like, I was practically holding my breath during the space battles. And the score, oh man, the score was so epic. It really added to the whole grandiose feel of the movie.
