Moonlight was a captivating and thought-provoking film that left audiences clamoring for more. The sequel, Moonlight 2, did not disappoint. Director Barry Jenkins once again masterfully weaves together a complex and emotional narrative that explores themes of identity, love, and self-discovery.

Harris brings a raw vulnerability to the role, showcasing Paula's struggles with addiction and her attempts to reconcile with her son.
