Glimmer is hands down one of the best movies I've seen in a long time. The plot was so intense, I was on the edge of my seat the entire time. The main character, Luna, played by the talented Scarlett Williams, was so relatable and real. I could feel every emotion she was going through, from her joy to her heartbreak. 

The special effects in this movie were out of this world. The way they created the Glimmer dimension was stunning. It felt like I was right there with Luna, experiencing everything she was experiencing. The way the colors shimmered and shifted was mesmerizing.

The supporting characters were all amazing as well. Luna's best friend, Jade, brought so much humor and heart to the film. And the villain, Shadow, was truly terrifying. I got chills every time he appeared on screen. 

But what really made this movie stand out was the message it delivered. It was all about finding your inner strength and believing in yourself, even when things seem impossible. Luna's journey was so inspiring, and it really made me think about my own life and struggles.
