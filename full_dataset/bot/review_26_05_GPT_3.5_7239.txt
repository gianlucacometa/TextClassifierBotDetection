The pacing of the movie was incredibly slow, with long, tedious scenes that added nothing to the overall plot. But perhaps the most egregious sin of Fading Journey was its lack of a cohesive storyline. The movie meandered aimlessly from one scene to the next, leaving the audience confused and disengaged. There was no clear direction or resolution, leaving viewers feeling unsatisfied and unfulfilled.

Overall, Fading Journey was a complete waste of time. I would not recommend this movie to anyone, as it failed to deliver on any level. Save yourself the trouble and skip this one altogether.
