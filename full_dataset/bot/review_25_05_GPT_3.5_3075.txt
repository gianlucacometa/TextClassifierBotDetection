The characters in "Forgotten Whispers" were so beautifully crafted, each with their own distinct personalities and motivations. Luna was a brave and curious protagonist, determined to uncover the truth and make things right. Her interactions with the other characters, such as the wise old owl and the mischievous sprites, added depth and heart to the story.

The vibrant colors and intricate details brought the world of forgotten whispers to life in a way that was truly mesmerizing. I found myself completely immersed in the world on screen, feeling as though I was right there with Luna on her journey.
