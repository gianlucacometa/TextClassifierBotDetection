"Lost Night" was an absolute rollercoaster of a movie from start to finish! 
The plot was so captivating, with twists and turns that I never saw coming. The storyline kept me on the edge of my seat the entire time, and I was completely engrossed in the characters' journey. 
The actors did an incredible job bringing their characters to life. The chemistry between the lead actors was electric, and their performances were so raw and emotional. I felt like I was right there with them, experiencing every moment alongside them. 
The cinematography was stunning. The way the scenes were shot added to the overall intensity of the film, and the use of lighting and color really helped set the mood. 
The soundtrack was also on point. The music really enhanced the emotions of each scene and added another layer to the storytelling. 
Overall, "Lost Night" is a must-watch for anyone who loves a good mystery thriller. It kept me guessing until the very end, and left me with so many thoughts and feelings. I can't wait to rewatch it and pick up on all the little details I may have missed the first time around. 10/10 would recommend!
