"The Spy Who Dumped Me" is a thrilling action-comedy that kept me on the edge of my seat the entire time. The movie follows Audrey and Morgan, two best friends who become entangled in a dangerous spy mission after Audrey's ex-boyfriend reveals he is a CIA agent.

Mila Kunis and Kate McKinnon have amazing on-screen chemistry, and their banter had me laughing out loud throughout the film. Their characters are relatable and endearing, adding a layer of humor and heart to the intense spy plot.

The action sequences in the movie are incredibly well done, with high-octane car chases, shootouts, and hand-to-hand combat. The stunts are jaw-dropping, and the special effects are top-notch.

The supporting cast also shines in this film, with standout performances from Justin Theroux as Audrey's mysterious ex-boyfriend and Sam Heughan as a charming MI6 agent. The twists and turns in the plot kept me guessing until the very end, and I was impressed by the clever writing and tight pacing.

Overall, "The Spy Who Dumped Me" is a fun and entertaining movie that is perfect for a night out with friends or family. It's a mix of comedy, action, and suspense that will leave you wanting more. I highly recommend this film to anyone looking for a good time at the movies.
